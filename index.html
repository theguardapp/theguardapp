About

Transparency you
can trust

The Guard was built from a simple belief: your device should work for you, not against you. As Android devices grow more powerful, so do the risks — from overheating to privacy leaks to silent battery drain.

We built The Guard to be the app we always wished existed — lightweight, honest, and relentlessly informative. No bloat. No subscriptions. No data sent to servers.
